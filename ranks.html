ABOUT RANKS
The essence of the ranks is to show you your level of responsibility, which depends on the number of working sets.

Если вы усердно трудились менее чем 20 сетов, то вы preschooler
From 20 to 100 - first grader
From 100 to 200 - schoolchild
From 200 to 300 - student
From 300 to 400 - bachelor
From 400 to 500 - master
From 500 to 600 - graduate student
From 600 to 700 - assistant professor
From 700 to 800 - professor
From 800 to 900 - academician
From 900 to 1000 - Катя
1000+ and more - Батя
